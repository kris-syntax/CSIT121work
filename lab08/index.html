Why did you choose this PNG Image?

I chose this particular image of an old school desk top computer for my png example because it shows its ability to incorporate transparent backgrounds. Using a png image with a transparent background really makes the details of the image really pop out .

Benefits of using PNG images

  • Transparency support
  • Lossless compression
  • High quality images
