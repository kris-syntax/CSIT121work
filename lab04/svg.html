Why did you choose this SVG image?

I chose this image because the vibrant colors help to show how versatile svg images are when resizing. Due to these types of images not using pixels the same way as other image types they do not get distorted or lose quality. When scaling this image to different sizes to test for my web page I found that the quality of all parts of the image stayed consistent and crisp as SVG images are described.

Benefits of using SVG images

  • They are scalable to different sizes
  • They can be optimized for search engines
  • Can be expanded without losing quality
