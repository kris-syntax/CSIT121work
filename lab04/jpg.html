Why did you choose this JPG image?

I chose this image of a sunflower because I feel it does a great job at showing how well photographs are interpreted. The colors in this photo are very vivid and the variation between color and texture captures great detail.

Benefits of using JPG Images

  1. Great for photographs
  2. Can use over 16 million colors
  3. Good for sharing images via email and social media
